Event Description

Embark on a thrilling journey through time and immerse yourself in the wonders of ancient civilizations. The "Ancient Adventure Quest"
invites you to step into the shoes of intrepid explorers and discover the secrets of the past.
Join us for a day filled with history, mystery, and excitement!

Event Highlights

  • Engaging guided tours through ancient civilizations
  • Interactive workshops on ancient art, crafts, and technologies
  • Ancient treasure hunt with exciting prizes
  • Live demonstrations of ancient rituals and traditions
  • Cosplay competition – Dress up as your favorite historical figure!
  • Delicious authentic food and beverages from different eras
  • And much more!
